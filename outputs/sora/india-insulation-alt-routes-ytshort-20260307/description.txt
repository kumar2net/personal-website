A 40-second Sora short on how India can reduce exposure to Middle East shocks.

Core idea: do not depend on one corridor. Build redundancy across oil supply, submarine cables, and cargo routes.

Visual beats:
- chokepoint risk around Hormuz, the Red Sea, and Suez
- wider crude sourcing plus bigger strategic reserves
- more cable landings and rerouting capacity
- cargo alternatives through Chabahar, Vladivostok, eastern gateways, and stronger Indian ports

Question: which backup matters most next for India: oil, data, or cargo?
