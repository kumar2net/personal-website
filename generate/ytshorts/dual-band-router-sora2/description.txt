Most homes suffer Wi-Fi lag because too many devices stay crowded on 2.4 GHz.
This short explains why dual-band routers (2.4/5 GHz) help split traffic and improve day-to-day performance.

Ask your broadband provider for:
- Simultaneous dual-band (2.4 + 5 GHz)
- Wi-Fi 5 (ac) or Wi-Fi 6 (ax)
- Gigabit WAN/LAN ports
- WPA2/WPA3 security
- 2x2 MU-MIMO + beamforming
- Optional mesh support
